Understanding Comics by Scott McCloud

Response by Amanda Martin


Within Understanding Comics by Scott McCould, one section that particularly stood out to me was Chapter Two, The Vocabulary of Comics. Right away, the message of this chapter became abundantly clear to me with each correction regarding the painting “The Treachery of Images” by Magritte.

Not only did this section encourage me to rethink what the description of this image should be with each following point that was made, but it also gave me a better understanding of perspective when viewing different art forms.



This comic also gave me a further look into different ways of communicating ideas and messages beyond the standard realm of explanations.

The icons that were displayed in this section especially gave me a stronger sense of what becomes entwined with words or images, such as “concepts, ideas and philosophies.”

Sun Signs
Just as the sun is the center point of our solar system, the sun signs represent the center aspects of a person. Your sun sign is one of twelve zodiacs that is based on the position of the Earth in relation to the sun on the day you were born. Because the sun has the strongest pull on the earth, sun signs influence people the most. Therefore, your sun sign can tell you the core characteristics of who you are. Astrologists say this sign signifies how you express yourself to the world.

The Sign's Quality:
Mutable
A sign's qualities correlate strongly to their relation to the seasons. The mutable signs all mark the end, or changing, of the seasons. Gemini season marks the end of spring leading into the start of summer, signaling the warm, bright, and adventurous aspects of this sign. However, their mutability also shows their readiness for change and their qualities of preparation and adaptability. Also, because they are the mutable air sign, geminis are especially known for their "airiness" as a key aspect of their claming, gentle, and comforting personalities.

Sun Signs
Just as the sun is the center point of our solar system, the sun signs represent the center of a person. Your sun sign is one of twelve zodiacs that is based on the position of the Earth in relation to the sun on the day you were born. Because the sun has the strongest pull on the earth, sun signs influence people the most. Therefore, your sun sign can tell you the core characteristics of who you are and how you express yourself to the world.
